Data and Interpretation Boundary

This applied-grade experiment uses mixed FMP/Yahoo market data and estimated market-cap-at-selection values. It is not a CRSP/WRDS-equivalent survivorship-free replication. Use it as evidence of an auditable financial NLP workflow, not a trading-alpha claim.
